About REVIVE Church

REVIVE Church exists to empower lives through the Gospel of Jesus Christ. Our pastor, Martin Lee, preaches world-class sermons every week that penetrait hearts all around the world. Today, we have 10 campuses, 9 in NZ and 1 in Nashville, TN. Our vision is “Empowering lives through the Gospel.”

Join us in person at any location or online at any time!.

YTH Ministry

Led by Pastor Michael Mireen, our YTH ministry hosts weekly YTH Nights, Rise Nights each term, and the annual YTHAscent summer camp (January 20-23rd, 2026). Teens can also join weekly Connect Groups online or in-person at their campuses.

The youth band leads worship on alternate Friday nights so that all our youth can experience Jesus.

REVIVE Music

Launched in July 2022, REVIVE Music has released four albums that are available anywhere you listen to music. The team tours every year and leads worship every Sunday.
